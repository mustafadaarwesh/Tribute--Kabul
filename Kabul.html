History and Beauty of Kabul

History

Kabul, Persian Kābol, city, capital of the province of Kabul and of Afghanistan. The largest urban centre in the country and also its political and economic hub, the city forms one of several districts of Kabul province.

Beauty

Kabul has multiple places to visit during the Spring and summer weather. people from around the world visit Kabul city to enjoy its weather. During the different era, Kabul was the center of empires, Tourist can visit the palaces built by different kings.

Weather and Entertainment

Weather

In Kabul, the summers are hot, dry, and clear and the winters are very cold, snowy, and partly cloudy. Over the course of the year, the temperature typically varies from 24°F to 93°F and is rarely below 14°F or above 99°F.

Entertainment

Kabul is a city full of entertainment. People can make the most of watching buz-kashi, Ghorsai and other national entertainment during the holiday seasons. You will have fund to watch these amazing and exciting games.
